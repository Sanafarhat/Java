Hexa-Decimal  Integer
---------------------------
1) What  is  the  prefix  of  hexa-decimal  number ? ---> 0X  (or)  0x

2) What  are  the  valid  characters  in  hexa-decimal  number ? ---> 0  to  9 , A  to  F  and  a  to  f

3) What  are  the  values  of  A  to  F ?  --->  10  to  15

4) What  is  the  base  of  hexa-decimal  number ?  ---> 6 + 10 = 16  due  to  10  digits  and  6  alphabets

5) System . out . println(Hexa-decimal-number);
    What  is  printed ?  ---> The  decimal  equivalent

6) int  x = 0XA7B9;
    What  does  variable  'x'  contain ?  ---> The  decimal  equivalent  i.e.  10 * 16 ^ 3 + 7 * 16 ^ 2 + 11 * 16 ^ 1 + 9 * 16 ^ 0

7) JVM  automatically  converts  hexa-decimal  number  to  decimal  number  which  is  assigned  to  the  variable

8) hex  dec  int  x = 0XA7B9;
    Is  the  statement  valid ?  ---> No  becoz  there  is  no  keyword  hex  dec
